Create unique memories with our seasonal treats

We are an artisan tea house from beautiful North Tyne Valley serving our own English green tea since the 1970s. Step inside and you will find a welcoming atmosphere where good quality and simplicity are paramount.

Our tearoom

Ceremony

There is no more archetypal English ritual than the ceremony of afternoon tea. Enjoy our specialty teas which are carefully selected by our tea experts to provide a delicious and authentic tea experience. Choose from our freshly made sandwiches, scones, cakes and many more. If needed, ask a member of staff for help choosing the right tea for you
